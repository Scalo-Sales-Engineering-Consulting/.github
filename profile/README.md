## Who We Are

[Scalo](https://scalosoft.com) is a European software development and IT consulting company with **650+ engineers**, **100+ clients across 15 countries**, and over **750 successful projects** delivered since our founding. Headquartered in Poland with offices in Wrocław, Warsaw, Kraków, Katowice, and Austin (TX), we partner with organizations worldwide to design, build, and scale custom software, AI & data platforms, and cloud solutions.

We hold **ISO 27001** certification, are a **Gold Microsoft Partner** and **Databricks Consulting Partner**, and have been recognized in the **Clutch Top 1000 Companies** list and as a **Forbes Diamond Award** recipient.

## What This Organization Is For

This GitHub organization is the **innovation workshop** of our Sales Engineering & Consulting practice. Here we:

- 🔬 **Build Proofs of Concept** — rapidly prototype solutions to validate ideas before they reach production.
- 🛡️ **Battle-Test Technologies** — evaluate frameworks, platforms, and architectural patterns under real-world conditions.
- 🚀 **Prove Out New Ideas** — explore emerging trends in AI/ML, cloud-native architectures, data engineering, and modern application development.
- 📚 **Extend Our Expertise** — deepen the hands-on knowledge and experience of our consultants so they can deliver even greater value to clients.

Every repository in this organization represents an experiment, a spike, or a proof of concept — a place where curiosity meets engineering rigor.
